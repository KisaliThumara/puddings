Recipe 3 : Caramel pudding

Ingredients

  • Eggs - 4 eggs
  • Suger - 1 cup
  • Milk - 2 cups
  • Vanila extract - 1 teaspoon

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 2: In a saucepan, melt sugar over medium heat until it turns into a golden caramel color. Pour the caramel into the bottom of a baking dish and tilt to coat the bottom evenly.

Step 3: In a bowl, beat the eggs. Add milk and vanilla extract, and mix well.

Step 4: Strain the egg mixture to remove any lumps and pour it over the caramel in the baking dish.

Step 5: Place the baking dish in a larger pan filled with hot water (water bath).

Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted into the center.

Step 7: Allow the caramel pudding to cool, then refrigerate for a few hours or overnight.

Step 8: Once chilled, run a knife along the edges, invert the pudding onto a serving plate, and let the caramel sauce flow over the top.

Slice and serve chilled. Enjoy your delicious caramel pudding!
