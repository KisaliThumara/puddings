Recipe 2 : Marble pudding

Ingredients

  • 10-12 Marie biscuits
  • Cocoa Powder - 2 tablespoon
  • Custard powder -2 tablespoon
  • Suger - 1/2 cup
  • Milk -2 cups
  • Vanila extract - 1 teaspoon

Cooking Instructions

Step 1: In a saucepan, heat 1.5 cups of milk. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.

Step 2: In a separate bowl, mix custard powder with the remaining 0.5 cup of cold milk to create a smooth paste.

Step 3: Slowly add the custard mixture to the hot milk, stirring continuously. Cook until the custard thickens. Add vanilla extract and mix well. Let it cool.

Step 4: Meanwhile, dip each biscuit in water for a second and layer them at the bottom of a serving dish.

Step 5: Once the custard has cooled, pour half of it over the biscuit layer.

Step 6: Add cocoa powder to the remaining custard, mix well to create chocolate custard.

Step 7: Pour the chocolate custard over the first layer in the dish.

Step 8: Use a skewer or knife to create a marbled effect by gently swirling through the layers.

Step 9: Refrigerate for at least 3 hours or until set.

Serve chilled and enjoy your simple and delicious marble pudding!
